Deep Learning for Echocardiography Image Segmentation

  • Developed a novel deep learning architecture integrating multi-attention mechanisms and a hybrid transformer bottleneck. This approach significantly improves the segmentation accuracy of complex echocardiography images. Achieved state-of-the-art results, published in Scientific Reports (Nature).

Optimizing Skin Cancer Diagnosis

  • Introduced an advanced methodology to enhance the accuracy of skin cancer diagnosis by leveraging late discrete wavelet transforms alongside novel swarm-based optimization algorithms. This work was published in Machine Learning with Applications.

EEG Analysis and Brain-Computer Interfaces

Conducted extensive preprocessing and classification of EEG signals from schizophrenia datasets using diverse machine and deep learning methods. Furthermore, developed highly accurate neural networks for automated sleep stage prediction based on EEG signal analysis, and performed source reconstruction of brain activity using Beamforming Techniques.

Advanced Video Analytics & Autonomous Driving

Implemented intelligent video analytics pipelines utilizing YOLOv8, DeepSORT, and ByteTrack for traffic analysis and Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR). Additionally, developed robust autonomous driving perception frameworks utilizing YOLOv8 on the KITTI Dataset.
